Buffalo Wings

Description

Need a quick appetizer for sunday night football? Then get ready for these tangy and zesty Buffalo wings. These wings are a perfect little snack that never have left overs after any occasion

Ingredients

  • 12 whole chicken wings
  • 3 ounces unsalted butter
  • 1 small clove garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup hot sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
